About Us

Who We Are

Welcome, I’m Ollie and I’ve had a passion for cleaning cars for over 20 years. Whilst for many of these years this was very much a hobby, my motivation changed slightly in 2015 when I won one of the prestigious National categories for concours with Porsche Club GB. Having others judge and recommend your work is extremely satisfying and this spurred me on to enhance and polish my skills further. Having had the privilege to work on a wide variety of vehicles from builders vans to supercars, I look forward to working with you to bring some shine back to your pride and joy.
